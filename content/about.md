Have you ever wanted to completely control your robot vacuum? When the first
ones came out they needed no internet connection and did not leak all your data
to the company that controls them, but now to even function they need an
internet connection. I want something better, something I can control, something
that only reports what I want it to. With this site I will reimplement a Shark
Matrix Robot Vacuum to work via a remote control and via a configured AWS
server.
